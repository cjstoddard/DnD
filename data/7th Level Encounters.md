## Encounter Notes

**Adjusted XP Calculation Method:**
- Solo encounters (1-10): Base XP × 1 = no multiplier
- Pairs (11-20, 21-25): Base XP × 1.5 multiplier
- Squads of 4 (26-30): Base XP × 2 multiplier
- Mixed compositions (31-50, 51-100): Base XP × 2.5 multiplier
- Swarms (79-85): Base XP × 1.5-2 multiplier (group difficulty)
- Boss encounters (99-100): Base XP × 1.5 multiplier

**Monster Selection:** All creatures drawn from the D&D 5E SRD with emphasis on variety: dragons, giants, elementals, undead, aberrations, and fiends. Encounters 51-100 feature advanced compositions including humanoid NPCs with class levels, incorporeal creatures, swarms, and unique named encounters.

**Scaling Notes:** These encounters assume standard player character resources. Groups with unusual builds (all damage dealers vs. heavy healing) may find some encounters easier or harder.
