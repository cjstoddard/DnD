## Treasure Notes

**Magic Item Distribution:**
- Very rare items (positions 25, 75): ***Portable Hole***, ***Cloak of Invisibility***
- Rare items (positions 10, 30, 50, 70, 90): **Wand of Fireballs**, **Staff of the Woodlands**, **Cape of the Mountebank**, **Amulet of Health**, **Deck of Illusions**
- Common/uncommon items (positions 5, 15, 20, 35, 40, 45, 55, 60, 65, 80): *Ring of Protection*, *Boots of Elvenkind*, *Cloak of Elvinkind*, *Goggles of Night*, *Immovable Rod*, *Rope of Climbing*, *Stone of Good Luck*, *Potion of Greater Healing*, *Ring of Water Walking*, *Deck of Illusions*

**Treasure Composition:** All treasures scaled to 7th-level party encounters (700-1,800 gp range) including coin denominations (cp, sp, ep, gp, pp) and thematic mundane items matching encounter types. Dragon hoards include precious gems and scales, undead lairs feature cursed items and wrappings, elemental encounters include magical shards and essence, and humanoid encounters include trophies and regalia.

**Encounter Matching:** Treasure names correspond exactly to the encounter names in [[7th Level Encounters]]. Use these tables together: roll d100 and reference the matching treasure number for your encounter.
